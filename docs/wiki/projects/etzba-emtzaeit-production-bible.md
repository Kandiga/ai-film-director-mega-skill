# אצבע אמצעית Production Bible

This page captures the durable directing logic for Netanel’s alien / middle-finger AI film world.

## Active visual anchors

- Phantom spacecraft: mystical musical vessel, not action warship.
- Teal-gold fracture planet: gradual mystery reveal.
- Resonating crystal palace: mystical / energy encounter.
- Alien Liberty Statue middle-finger beacon: iconic cultural, political, comedic, rebellious symbol.
- Roe alien: tonal heart / rebel / comic anchor.
- Market cultures: distinct societies, not a generic crowd.

See [[blueprint-first-production]] and [[cine-lock]].

## Middle-finger symbol

The finger is not just a gag. It can function as rebellion, freedom, sacred joke, cosmic insult, anti-authoritarian myth, or cultural beacon.

## Gesture QA

For middle-finger shots:

- one central raised middle finger;
- index/ring/little folded;
- thumb locking them;
- no torch;
- no index pointing;
- no open hand.

## Scene 0 / prequel options

1. Mythology of the finger — a crude gesture becomes a sacred civilization symbol.
2. Cosmic broadcast — the statue/finger beacon emits a red-gold pulse that the Phantom hears.
3. Roe in the market — introduce the tonal heart before arrival.
4. Sacred joke — a private/cosmic joke elevated into mythology.

## Practical pipeline

Approved blueprint / asset → clean 16:9 start frame → still QA → Seedance/Kling animation → ffprobe/contact-sheet/blackdetect QA → organized delivery folder and concise report.
